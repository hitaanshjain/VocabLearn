## Screen Overview

### Login

The login screen allows returning users to sign in with their account credentials and access their saved vocabulary data.

### Register

The register screen allows new users to create an account so their vocabulary words and quiz progress can be saved.

### Home

The home screen serves as the main landing page after a user signs in. It gives users access to the main features of the app.

### Word Bank

The word bank screen displays the user’s saved vocabulary words. Users can review their words and track their learning progress.

### Add Word

The add word screen allows users to manually add a new vocabulary word to their personal word bank.

### Search

The search screen allows users to look up words or use the reverse dictionary feature to find words based on a definition or prompt.

### Quiz

The quiz screen tests users on words from their word bank and helps reinforce vocabulary through repetition.

### Quiz Results

The quiz results screen shows feedback after a quiz attempt and helps users understand their progress.
